Create Links Over Your Image

  1. Click and drag your mouse over the section of the image you want to have a link.
    Left Click: Link opens in same window.
    Right Click: Link opens in new window.
  2. Double-click on one of your selected areas to set the URL for your link.
  3. Drag or resize your selections with the mouse or keyboard shortcuts.

Move: Arrow Keys, Remove: Delete Key, Adjust Height: Ctrl/Shift, Adjust Width: +/-
